OUR CLIENTELE

Our Clientele spans the Public and Private sectors of the Nigerian economy and extends beyond the shores of Nigeria. Armed with extensive local know-how, we ensure legal support in all areas of commercial law and guarantee timely and constructive advice to our Clients and proactive guidance. The firm has worked (and still works) for Ship Owners, Protection & Indemnity (P&I) Clubs, P&I Correspondent, Banks, Financial Institutions, Receivers of cargo, Major Underwriters, Oil and Gas multinationals, Courier multinationals, Corporate Organizations and Governmental parastatals We have also worked in association with various prestigious international law firms. Thus we humbly believe we are best equipped to handle and effectively represent the diverse and peculiar interests of any kind of Client.

OUR PHILOSOPHY

The interest of our individual Clients is uppermost and lies at the very centre of our being. We practice the core values of the legal profession such as confidentiality, trustworthiness, diligence and most importantly, a high level of professional conduct. Our team of lawyers, comprised of both old and new “wigs”, is modern and dynamic in spirit. We are flexible and continuously developing to adapt to constantly changing business climates and yet remain resilient in our goal, which is to deliver highly skilled and individually tailored support. We listen to our Clients, act upon their needs and deliver successfully. In short, we strive to offer the very best.
